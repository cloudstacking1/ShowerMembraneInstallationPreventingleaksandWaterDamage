The Purpose of a Shower Membrane

The shower membrane is one of the most critical components in any modern bathroom. It’s a hidden shield beneath your tile that prevents water from seeping into the foundation of your home. Without it, moisture can cause wood rot, mold infestations, and expensive repairs. With it, you get a watertight system designed for decades of safe and healthy use.
